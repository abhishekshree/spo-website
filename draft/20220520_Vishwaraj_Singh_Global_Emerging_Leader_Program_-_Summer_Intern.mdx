## Some resources that you used during your preparation?

## Can you list down the the topics on which questions were asked in the interviews or any other insights on the interview process?
Questions were based on general understanding of basics concepts of chemical engineering.
Little knowledge of practical application of Thermodynamics will be helpful. 
Thorough understanding of projects and internships written on resume is must. Questions on the core projects and their applications are asked.
## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
Generally have a specific profile in your mind you are targeting and you can prepare for the specific companies coming for that profile.
Be confident with your preparation and your basic concepts in whichever profile you are sitting and the process will be very relaxing.
Don't take tension as internships are the not the end goal for you.
Be confident in the interview, that will help you a lot.
