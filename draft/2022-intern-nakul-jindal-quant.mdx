## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
For software/analyst/quant roles - practice coding (DSA) along your semester so that in the summers before the interviews, you can prepare company-specific as each company has different requirements. 
To get your resume shortlisted often - High CPI
Techno-managerial - variety of projects and good POR is a plus
