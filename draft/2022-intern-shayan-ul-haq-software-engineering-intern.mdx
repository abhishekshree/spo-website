## Other companies & profiles that you were shortlisted for:
DE Shaw,(Software Developer Intern) and  Optiver (Software Engineer)

## Some resources that you used during your preparation?
Leetcode,"Test from Archives " Contests by Programming Club and contests on Codeforces

## Can you list down the the topics on which questions were asked in the interviews or any other insights on the interview process?
Google : 
->2 technical rounds of interview were held ( 45 min each), which consisted of 1-2 coding questions based on Data Structures and Algorithms.
->Candidates were required to share their approach and write the code in their preferred language on google docs 
->Nothing related to resume/projects was asked

DE Shaw:
->2 Technical rounds of interview  
->Coding Questions based on Data Structure and Algorithms 
and questions on Object Oriented Programming were asked 

Optiver:

->1 HR (Standard HR Questions)+ 1 Technical Round(Around 45 min discussion on "How are decimals represented in binary format ?", no coding /CS question !) 

## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
(Only relevant to SWE profiles )
->Start Early !
->Focus more  on Data Structures and Algorithms
->Questions on Leetcode will give you the flavour , that you can expect during Tests and Interviews
->Do participate in contests conducted by Programming Club
-> A decent rank in Google Kick-start might help you in getting shortlisted for the interviews (Only Relevant to Google )
->Some companies look for students with Competitive programming Profile. 
->Practice writing code on Google Docs
