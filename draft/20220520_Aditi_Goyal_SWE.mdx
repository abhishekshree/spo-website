## Some resources that you used during your preparation?
Interviewbit, Test Series by PClub, Codeforces contests, Leetcode previous year interview questions

## Can you list down the the topics on which questions were asked in the interviews or any other insights on the interview process?
Questions were based on arrays, dynamic programming, graph algorithms. Had to continuously explain the thought process to the interviewer.
## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
Even if you think that the algorithm that you have thought is not efficient in terms of space/time complexities, mention the algorithm. Interviewer will try to give you hints in case it can be improved. Work along with the interviewer, explaining each step.
