## Some resources that you used during your preparation?
Interviewbit, Leetcode for general practice and brainstellar and gurmeet's blog for quant puzzles.

## Can you list down the the topics on which questions were asked in the interviews or any other insights on the interview process?
Data structure and Algorithms(4-5 questions), 1 puzzle problem, Questions based on resume and some general HR type questions.
## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
For preparation, try to practice well beforehand (not at the end of summers). Try to keep yourself motivated throughout the process.

For interviews, be confident in your answers and your answers should reflect your confidence and optimism, checkout frequently asked questions in interview. And the last point is, if you are stuck ask for a hint. (For better guidance, be interactive with interviewer such that he/she has some idea how you approach the problem and can help you accordingly)
