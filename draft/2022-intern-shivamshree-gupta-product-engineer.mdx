## Other companies & profiles that you were shortlisted for:
Microsoft Engage - Software Development, Goldman Sachs - Analyst, Google - Software  

## Some resources that you used during your preparation?
Platforms for Competitive Programming -  Interviewbit, Leetcode, Codeforces, GeeksforGeeks
For puzzles - Brainstellar, Fifty- challenging problems

## Can you list down the the topics on which questions were asked in the interviews or any other insights on the interview process?
Most of the topics asked in the interviews - String, Graph, Tree, Dynamic Programming , Binary Search


## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
Practice of questions is important, try to solve as many problems you can because this helps building the problem solving ability. Also, most of the interviews are discussion based so while practising try to discuss with your friends. This brings out several possibilities of seeing the same problem, and will make interviews feel like just another discussion.
