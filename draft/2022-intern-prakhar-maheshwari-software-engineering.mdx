## Other companies & profiles that you were shortlisted for:
Google Hardware: core, Graviton: quant, ITC: FMCG

## Some resources that you used during your preparation?
Geeks For Geeks, Interview Bit, Brainstellar

## Can you list down the the topics on which questions were asked in the interviews or any other insights on the interview process?
mental mathematics, logical ability, computer science fundamentals, system design problems

The questions asked were relatively simple as compared to the other companies but they were looking for reason/logic for any approach or method you used to solve the question.

For system design question, provide reason for any choice you make and consider all the possible factors due to which your choice may not work.

## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
Have a good understanding of computer science fundamentals, some knowledge of system design would be a plus. Provide a readable code during both the technical test as well as the interview.
