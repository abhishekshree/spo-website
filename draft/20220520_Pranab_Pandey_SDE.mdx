## Can you list down the the topics on which questions were asked in the interviews or any other insights on the interview process?
There were 3 rounds. 2 technical, 1 HR. 3 coding questions were asked in the first round covering topics of two pointers, 1d dp, finding least positive integer not present in the array. The second round consisted of 2 questions. A variation of longer common substring and a variation of longest common sub-sequence. In the first round, code execution was expected to pass all the test cases given by the interviewer. HR round was a casual one.
## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
Practice as many questions as you can before the coding rounds. After getting shortlisted for the interviews, focus only on the generic questions.
