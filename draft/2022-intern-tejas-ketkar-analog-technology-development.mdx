## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
Target a specific role, set a goal and work towards that. Talk to numerous seniors, alums, and relevant people in the industry/academia. Use LinkedIn to your advantage. If you can't figure out what role to target then pick anything randomly and try to get some exposure in it before your SPO internship season. That will tell you whether you want to pursue that further or not. Best of luck!
