## Other companies & profiles that you were shortlisted for:
Global Market Analyst at Nomura

## Some resources that you used during your preparation?
InterviewBit for coding; Heard on the street, 50 Challenging Problems in Probability, and A Practical Guide to Quantitative Finance for puzzles and probability & statistics; Hands-on Machine Learning by Aurelien Geron for ML; FRM part 1 books and Investopedia for Finance.

## Can you list down the the topics on which questions were asked in the interviews or any other insights on the interview process?
There were two rounds of interviews. The first one revolved around puzzles, probability & statistics, and questions on Machine Learning topics like clustering, regularisation, etc. The second round focused on my resume, some basic statistics questions and basic HR questions.
## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
Don't waste your time and utilise the summer before the internship season properly. Be confident and don't lose hope throughout the internship season as a lot of factors are also dependent on luck during this time.
