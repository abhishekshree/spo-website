## Can you list down the the topics on which questions were asked in the interviews or any other insights on the interview process?

Try to not panic even if you cant answer.

## Any specific advice that you have for students sitting for internship next year?
Practice cp. This is the most important thing. Use leetcode for practicing basics of any algorithm/data structure, and then give contests on codeforces. If you want to crack any day 1 interview you must get good at codeforces. Master basics of statistical distributions and probability. Practice OOPS questions, some companies also ask dbms questions in tests.
