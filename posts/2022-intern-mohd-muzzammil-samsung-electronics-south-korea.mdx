his summer internship in Associate profile at SAMSUNG Electronics South Korea. Here are some brief
insights of his.

## Shortlisted Profiles & Companies:
Graviton, Systems, Quadeye, Systems

## Insights on the Selection Process
__Coding Round__
- All CSE and EE people were eligible
- Given a single problem and 3hrs to solve it.
- Largely a brute force question, with some little dp.

__CPI Shortlist__
- 14 people were shortlist after coding round based on CPI

__Interview__
- Coding Interview:  Easy question based on a variant of maximum subset problem.
- HR Interview: They basically try to assess how well may you fit the Korean Culture.
