## Insights on the Selection Process
In the American Express and MasterCard shortlist was done on the
basis OA, there were 2 rounds of interviews, questions based on
prob and stats, guesstimates, resume projects , basic ML
questions, what does the company , were asked .
LimeChat shortlist was based on resume and OA, having
experience ML and DL helps in shortlisting.
Walmart recruitment was totally based on the 3 OA rounds.
Other insights on the interview process -
Think out loud, the interviewer wants to know your approach.
Communicate well, try to catch the hints that the interviewer gave,
Think of the corner cases while solving problems.
Trying minimizing the use of Complex words.
In the end ask questions from the interviewer to show your
inquisitiveness.

## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
Start Your Preparation as early as possible.
Practice enough questions to gain confidence in topics.
Prepare your resume thoroughly well before in advance.
Don’t neglect the HR interview preparation, look at the “About the Company “section before
the interviews.
It may be possible that you might get rejected in interviews, don’t get demotivated Have Faith
in God, and continue your preparation, in the end you would grab an offer.
Talk to seniors in case of advice during the preparation and before the interviews. Feel free to

get in touch with me in case of any doubts.
