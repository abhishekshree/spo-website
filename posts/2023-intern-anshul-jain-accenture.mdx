##  Preparation Resources
Victor Cheng’s video series and case interview cracked( book) are the commonly used
resources for consulting prep, although no case was asked in the interview and only a
guesstimate. The rest was to read about Accenture (core values, etc.) before the interview for
HR questions.

## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
Practice your intro; read about companies before their interviews,after you are shortlisted.
Practice speaking in front of the mirror to gain confidence. Basic consulting prep will get
you through this interview no other prep is required.
