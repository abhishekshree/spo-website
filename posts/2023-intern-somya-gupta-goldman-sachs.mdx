Somya Gupta, studying in IIT Kanpur as a BS student in MTH will do
her summer internship in Summer Analyst profile at Goldman Sachs. Here are some brief
insights of hers.

## Shortlisted Profiles & Companies:
Google SDE, Goldman Sachs

## Insights on the Selection Process
1. Resume Shortlist:- {No such pattern is observed of cpi or branch}
2. TEST:- Lot of aptitude problems and two medium coding questions
3. Informal discussion with the employees before the interviews
{Put your best foot forward by attempting to engage everyone and ask
thoughtful questions that will help them remember you}
4. Interview:- 3 rounds of offline interviews, each of about 45 min, in
which we have to write pseudo codes for problems basically.

Sample Interview
/Test Questions

Interview
Round 1:- Coding questions of 2 pointer on array and string along with
thier time complexity and optimization if can be done and some general
conversations.
Round 2:- basic question on dp and like Fibonacci using dp approach
Graph question on BFS and DFS and conceptual question on it as well
Round 3:- General question on aptitude along with hr question like
why do want to join a finance firm and why do you want to join this

company(PS- Do a little-research about the company).
Then they also asked questions from my resume on any major project
of your choice in depth.

## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
Dos:-
Speak out your thoughts loud to the interviewer to he understands your thinking approach.
Maintain your confidence and avoid showing signs of anxiousness.
In the Hr round, don&#39;t pretend to be anyone and be yourself.

Don’ts:-
Never, ever skip the informal round prior to the interview.
