## Insights on the Selection Process
The test was average but the number of questions were more as
compared to the time given. In the interview process, the puzzle
questions asked were directly from brain stellar. They mostly
grilled on the projects mentioned and the work that AmEx does. I
had two finance related projects.

## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
1.Prepare well for the profile you are interested in and have in depth knowledge of the
projects that you’ve written.
2.Prepare coding basics for sure as many companies ( Might not be sde) ask coding
questions in tests and interviews.
3. Be confident and believe in yourself. Sometimes, confidence is the only key to crack an
interview.
4. Do not forget to prepare for HR Questions. They are equally important.
5. Do not get disheartened if you don’t bag an internship in first week or a month. You’ll
definitely succeed if you remain consistent.
