Himanshu Sood, studying in IIT Kanpur as a BT student in CSE will do
his summer internship in Quantitative Trading profile at Jane Street. Here are some brief
insights of his.

## Shortlisted Profiles & Companies:
Jane Street - Quant Trading, Quadeye - Quant, Microsoft - SDE

## Insights on the Selection Process
1. Resume Shortlist
2. Test - There was a subjective test with questions on probability
3. Interview Process - There were a total of five interviews, one of which was a preliminary round, and the other four happened consecutively on the final day.

They mostly tested for knowledge and ability to apply fundamental probability concepts such as linearity of expectation, understanding of probability distributions, etc.

##  Preparation Resources
A Collection of Dice Problems by Matthew M. Conroy, 50 Challenging Problems in Probability, Brainstellar, LeetCode, CSES Problemset

## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
For Quant profiles, it’s important to be well versed with Probability concepts (such as
expected value and linearity of expectation). They also expect you to be able to do simple
computations quickly and accurately.
