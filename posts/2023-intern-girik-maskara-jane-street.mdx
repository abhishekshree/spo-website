Girik Maskara, studying in IIT Kanpur as a BT student in CSE will do
his summer internship in Quantitative Trading profile at Jane Street. Here are some brief
insights of his.

## Shortlisted Profiles & Companies:
Jane Street - Quant Trading, Optiver - Quant Trading.

## Insights on the Selection Process
For Jane Street :
1. Resume Shortlist
2. Test - There was a subjective test with questions on probability
3. Interview Process - There were a total of four interviews, one of which was a preliminary round, and the other three happened consecutively on the final day.

They mainly test your knowledge and application to apply basic concepts of probability like expected value, linearity of expectation, understanding of probability distributions, etc.. 

##  Preparation Resources
